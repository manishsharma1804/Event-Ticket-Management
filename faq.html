Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventTix

How do I get my tickets after purchase?

After successful purchase, your tickets will be:
  • Immediately available in your EventTix account
  • Sent to your registered email address
  • Accessible through the mobile app

Can I transfer my tickets to someone else?

Yes, you can transfer tickets through your account dashboard. However, some events may have restrictions on ticket transfers. Check the specific event's terms for details.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:
  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Follow the instructions in the reset email

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • UPI
  • Net Banking
  • Digital Wallets

What happens if an event is cancelled?

If an event is cancelled:
  • You will be notified via email
  • Full refund will be processed automatically
  • Refund will be credited within 5-7 business days
