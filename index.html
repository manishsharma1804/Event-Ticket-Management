About EventTix

Your trusted partner in event ticketing

Our Story

EventTix was founded with a vision to revolutionize event ticketing in India. We understand the passion and excitement that comes with attending live events, and we're here to make that experience seamless and memorable.

Our platform serves millions of event enthusiasts across India, connecting them to a diverse range of events from cultural festivals to sporting events, concerts to comedy shows.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless and secure ticketing platform that connects event organizers with their audience, making event discovery and booking accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and preferred event ticketing platform, setting new standards in customer experience and technological innovation.
