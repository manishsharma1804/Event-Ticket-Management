Refund Policy

Last updated: Dec 2024

General Policy

EventTix strives to ensure fair and transparent refund processes for all our users. Our refund policy varies based on the event type and timing of the refund request.

Refund Eligibility Timeline

7+ Days Before Event

Full refund available, minus processing fees

3-7 Days Before Event

50% refund available, minus processing fees

Less than 72 Hours

No refund available except in special circumstances

Important: Some events may have their own specific refund policies. Please check the event details page for event-specific policies.

How to Request a Refund

1

Log into Your Account

Access your EventTix account and navigate to your tickets.

2

Select the Ticket

Choose the ticket you wish to refund from your purchases.

3

Submit Request

Fill out the refund request form with required details.

4

Wait for Confirmation

We'll process your request within 3-5 business days.

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions about refunds.
